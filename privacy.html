Clearing your browser storage will permanently remove saved CV information.

9. POPIA Explained (Plain Language)

POPIA is South Africa’s data-protection law that ensures personal information is collected lawfully, transparently, and securely.

Under POPIA, you have the right to:

  • Know what data is collected and why
  • Access or correct personal information
  • Request deletion where applicable
  • Expect reasonable safeguards

Easy Opt supports these principles by minimizing data collection and prioritizing user transparency.
